To navigate through Recipe Book, the 'View' menu is used, located at the top of the application's window. By selecting a specific view, the application will display all the relevant information.

The Ingredient view will display all the ingredients that your user profile has. There is a default list of ingredients that is also created when your account is created. A search bar on the main page allows you to quickly find whatever ingredient you're looking for. The create button lets you enter new ingredients to use in your recipes.

The Recipe view will display all the recipes that your user profile has created, as well as all the shared recipes your profile has liked. A search bar on the main page allows you to quickly find your recipes by name.

The Community Hub view allows you to see all the recipes shared by other users. They are free to browse and save; adding them to your own recipes for easier access.
